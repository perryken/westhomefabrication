WEST HOME FABRICATION

Your Trusted Partner for Quality Aluminium Products in Ghana, Accra

West Home Fabrication is a leading aluminium fabrication company in Accra, Ghana, specializing in high-quality aluminium doors, windows, showcases, balustrades, and Alucobond cladding. With a commitment to precision and excellence, we provide durable, stylish, and functional solutions for residential and commercial properties.
Our team of skilled fabricators and designers combines advanced craftsmanship with premium materials to deliver customized aluminium installations that enhance aesthetics, security, and efficiency. Whether it’s modern architectural designs or bespoke fabrication projects, we ensure unmatched quality and reliability.
With a strong reputation built on expertise, innovation, and customer satisfaction, West Home Fabrication remains the trusted choice for superior aluminium solutions in Accra, Ghana.
